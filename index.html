Music update

New stuff today. The update is music themed, I got some new doujin(同人) albums and I uploaded two of them. You can get them by clicking the designated links. I didn't include any scans with the upload but I could update that in the future. Things that are missing are back cover arts for both albums and "Spoon" also has a booklet with song lyrics in Japanese, credits and some other stuff.

Spoon - Eterna Musik

This one is called "Spoon", it's a very soothing and cheerful album. It has 14 tracks and they're all vocal songs. They keep a very pleasant athmosphere throughout the entire album. There are a lot of different vocalists in this album as well. My favorite songs are "Rhythm" and "Mirai no michi he" (roughly: Towards the road to the future). This album is undoubtedly moe and it's great if you like the idea of being charmed by fictional characters, I might translate the songs from this album in the future.

Wish You Smilin'! - M.I.Factory(Megumi Iseebi)

Wish you smilin'! Wonderful album with a lot of retro game sounding music and an amazing vocal song "卒業" (graduation) sung by NAO. This album reminds me a lot of "しゃいんぶるむ" (Shine bloom) also by M.I.Factory but with it's own unique feel, sadly no bonus track in this one unlike "Shine Bloom".

OMAKE!!! (bonus)

Omake! (written as おまけ)! Just a Japanese word for "bonus". I made this drawing today on paper. I thought it looks very cute so I included it in the update. I tried going with JULIET sleeves for extra MOE!! Regrettably I couldn't fit the entire hand in the shot, I think it would've made it look a lot better. Regardless, I hope this drawing gives you moe! That is it for this week's update, I hope it was at least a little amusing. If you'd like to hear more about similar music in the future let me know!
